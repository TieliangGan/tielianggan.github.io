I am currently a student in University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and doing my Master's Degree in Shanghai Institute of Organic Chemistry, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I obtained my Bachelor's Degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2021. During the study of my Bachelor's Degree, I also did an exchange program in EPFL. So far, I have studied in three different institutes in Chinese Academy of Sciences, which are Institute of Zoology (IOZ), Institue of Chemistry (ICCAS), and Shanghai Institute of Organic Chemistry (SIOC).

My research interests mainly include cell signals, molecular sociology and 4D molelcular physiology by approaches like structure biology by cryo-EM and cryo-ET and biochemical characterizations.

I am especially interested in taking advantage of my knowledge in calculus, linear algebra, statistics, physics and computer skills to explore the multi-disciplinary field of life science. For example, using machine learning libraries to do data mining, making the most of the excessive data online.
